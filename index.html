Bird Conservation

This website is about bird conservation.

About

On this page you will learn all about various types of birds, their history, habits and protection.

Protection of our birds is important. For example, in Ireland some of the birds protected are:

  • Barn owl
  • Hen harrier
  • Yellowhammer
  • Curlew

Threats to birds:

  1. Habitat destruction
  2. Pollution
  3. Climate change
